About Us

At Consent Ed, we immerse you in interactive games that simulate real-life experiences. These games help you navigate tricky situations, all while learning about healthy, consensual interactions.

As you progress, you’ll collect a verification badge that represents your learning. These badges show others that you are educated on consent and committed to respectful, safe interactions.

We believe that games don’t just entertain—they can help reinforce good behaviors and create positive social change.
