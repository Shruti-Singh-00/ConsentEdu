Just like you control who sees your posts or sends you messages on social media, consent in real life operates similarly.....

You decide who gets access, when, and how much.....

At Consent Ed, we help you understand that, just like adjusting your online privacy settings, your personal boundaries are yours to control —and they can change as you feel comfortable.

When someone disregards those boundaries, it's like bypassing your privacy settings without permission —it’s a violation

Consent Ed teaches you to communicate your boundaries clearly and ensures others respect them —just like respecting someone’s privacy online.
