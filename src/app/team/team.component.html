Job Title-2

Growing up in India, and even after moving to the US, I’ve been deeply disturbed by the prevalence of sexual assault and the fear it instills in women around the world. When I learned that 6% of college-age men admit to committing acts that meet the legal definition of rape—and yet 88% don’t believe their actions qualify as rape—I realized there’s a critical gap in education that must be addressed. That’s why I founded Consent Ed: to provide effective, meaningful sex education that addresses this urgent need.
